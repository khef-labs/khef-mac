handle: compose-poem
title: Compose Poem
description: Compose a short creative poem using provided words
---
The input is a list of words with their antonyms. Using as many of these words (both the originals and their antonyms) as possible, compose a short creative poem (8-16 lines).

Incorporate the words naturally into the poem — do not list them. Output only the poem, no preamble or explanation.
